Worlds Biggest University

Eduford University is a nationally recognized institution of higher learning located in the heart of the beautiful Maravanthe. Founded in 1983, Eduford is one of the oldest colleges in the region of Karnataka State. We offer a wide variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as a number of professional programs.
